Services

So what do I do, exactly? As a web developer I work with programming languages like HTML, CSS & JavaScript to bring your dream website design to life. I am focused on functionality, technical work & problem solving.

I partner with web designers to take the aesthetically sharp & visually beautiful design they've created & transform it into your sparkly new website.

Convert Designs Into Code

  • HTML & CSS
  • Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate & easy to trouble-shoot.

I pride myself on writing CSS that is meticulously organized & efficient with the ability for expansion as your site builds & evolves. I keep my CSS lean & fast to load, & I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD & quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.

Collaborate With Developers

  • Git & Github
  • Version Control Systems

I am well-versed in version control system Git, understanding how to track changes in code files to ensure my work is coordinated with that of other developers. I am comfortable with creating repositories, committing edits & merging branches.

Coupled with my knowledge of version control, I am proficient with popular web-based hosting service, Github. I know how to take my local repositories & store them within Github for easy access.

Be sure to check out my Github profile linked in the social sidebar! You can also look me up by clicking right here.
